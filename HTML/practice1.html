Mark Ruffalo

Mark

Mark Ruffalo is a famous actor known for his role in the avengers movies as the incredible hulk. He is also an activist and supports a number of causes like anti-fracking, renewable energies, pro-choice movement, and LGBTQ rights. He also founded the solutions project to solve such problems.
